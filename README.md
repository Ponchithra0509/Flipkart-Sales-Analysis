## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes Flipkart sales, products, customer ratings, pricing, and seller performance using SQL and Power BI.

The objective is to transform raw business data into interactive dashboards that provide meaningful business insights for decision-making.

# 🎯 Business Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze Flipkart sales data and transform raw business data into meaningful insights using SQL and Power BI.

This dashboard helps answer important business questions related to:

* Revenue Performance
* Product Categories
* Brand Performance
* Customer Ratings
* Price Segments
* Seller Cities
* Monthly Sales Trends

# 📊 Dashboard Pages

## 1️⃣ Executive Dashboard

Provides an overall business summary using KPIs and high-level insights.

Includes:

* Total Revenue
* Total Units Sold
* Total Products
* Average Rating
* Revenue by Category
* Revenue by Brand
* Revenue by Price Category

---

## 2️⃣ Sales Analysis

Analyzes sales trends and performance over time.

Includes:

* Monthly Revenue Trend
* Revenue by Seller City
* Revenue by Category
* Top Performing Brands

---

## 3️⃣ Product & Brand Performance

Evaluates product quality and brand performance.

Includes:

* Brand Revenue
* Product Ratings
* Category Performance
* Price Category Analysis

---

## 4️⃣ Interactive Insights

Interactive dashboard with filters for detailed business exploration.

Available slicers:

* Year
* Month
* Category
* Brand
* Seller City
* Price Category

---

# 📈 Key Business Insights

* Premium products generated the highest revenue.
* A small number of brands contributed a significant share of total sales.
* Monthly sales trends revealed seasonal variations.
* Seller city performance varied considerably across regions.
* Customer ratings helped identify top-performing products.
